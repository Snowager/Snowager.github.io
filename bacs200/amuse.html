Amusement #1 - Open Constructor

Open Constructor is a github recreation of the old webtoy SodaPlay.

SodaPlay was an engineering tool that allowed you to create moving creatures out of vector lines.

Through Peter Fidelmen's github you can experience it again.

Click here to build a monstrosity

Amusement #2 - Bob and George

Bob and George is an old comic strip that helped popularize videogame sprite comics.

Having been completed several years ago, it is several thousand comic strips long.

Click here to start from the beginning.

Amusement #3 - Line Rider

Line Rider is a game where you draw lines out on the screen and then have a little figure ride across them.

Line Rider is immensely popular, and even now has mobile versions you can play on your phone.

There are plenty of well-made complex art pieces people have made on Youtube that are line ride tracks.

Click here to make your line.
